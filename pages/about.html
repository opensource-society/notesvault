About NotesVault

NotesVault is a community-driven, beginner-friendly, open-source platform designed to help students organize, share, and access academic notes and Previous Year Questions (PYQs) in a sustainable way.

Mission Statement

To democratize learning by providing a centralized, collaborative space for students to share curriculum-aligned learning material and eliminate academic resource silos.

Vision

To make academic resources easily discoverable, universally accessible, and empowering for every student—regardless of institution or background.

How NotesVault Helps Students

  • Unified Resource Hub: All notes and PYQs in one place—no more scattered links or chats.
  • Smart Search: Navigate with tags, filters, and semesters.
  • Collaborative Uploads: Anyone can upload and contribute content.
  • Stress-Free Exam Prep: Forget last-minute panic. Quickly find trusted resources.
